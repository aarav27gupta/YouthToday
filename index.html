About Us

YouthToday is a unique, youth-founded and youth-run platform dedicated to empowering the voices of the next generation. We are a quarterly publication that provides a space for young people to express their thoughts, creativity, and opinions through articles, reviews, artwork, photography, etc. At YouthToday, we believe in the power of the youth to shape society and inspire change. YouthToday is not just a newspaper, it is a step towards creating an informed and engaged youth for a better tomorrow.
